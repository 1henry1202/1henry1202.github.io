Competitions

SoCal Cyber Cup

The SoCal Cyber Cup is a regional cybersecurity competition hosted in Southern California. It focuses on teamwork, defense strategies, and protecting simulated computer networks from attacks. High school and college students collaborate to solve security challenges and earn points based on their performance.

CyberPatriot

CyberPatriot is a national youth cyber education program created by the Air Force Association. The main competition tasks teams with finding and fixing cybersecurity vulnerabilities in virtual operating systems. It helps students build real-world defensive skills, teamwork, and technical expertise while competing nationwide.
