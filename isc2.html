ISC2

(ISC)² – International Information System Security Certification Consortium – is one of the world’s most influential cybersecurity certification bodies. Its CISSP credential is widely regarded as the “gold standard”, recognized in 180+ countries.

Why it matters

For students aiming at cybersecurity, IT, or related management roles, ISC2 certifications validate comprehensive security knowledge and can open doors to high‑level positions in government agencies, enterprises, and multinationals.

Global recognition

The UK considers CISSP comparable to Master’s degree level. Across the U.S., EU, and APAC, many organizations list CISSP or SSCP as preferred or required credentials.

Pathway for students

  • SSCP – good for fundamentals and security operations.
  • CC (Certified in Cybersecurity) – entry level, great for first certification.
  • CISSP – leadership/architect role for experienced professionals.
