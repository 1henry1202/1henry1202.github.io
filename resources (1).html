Realistic boxes for advanced practice and team scrims. Focus on recon, privilege escalation and persistence chains.

Classic Linux & networking wargames (e.g., Bandit). Builds command‑line fluency and problem‑solving foundations.

Authoritative web‑security curriculum covering OWASP Top 10, from fundamentals to advanced exploitation & defenses.
